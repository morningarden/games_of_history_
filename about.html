About Me and This Page

I teach global history in a high school. My students seem to have difficulty imaging and realising old things. In such case, using games can change the atmosphere.

About School where I work

This is a high school at the normal Japanese academic level. The students don't like to study much, but they are very honest and are happy just to play games.

Why I Suggest to Use Games

Students can learn deeper !

The well-crafted world of the board game allows students to relive history.

Students can enjoy learning !

Students enjoy a game, no matter how difficult it is. They lose track of time.
