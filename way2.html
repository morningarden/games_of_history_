After Learning is complete

On this page I present games that can be used as a review after all textbook coverage has been completed. It puts smiles back on the faces of students who are tired from studying for exams.

    ◆◇GALLERY◇◆

    Sorting Game

  • >>Click for larger image<<

    • TYPE: card game
    • LEVEL: easy
    • TIME: 30min
    • You can sort chronologically. We can also make own cards.
    • The rules of this game are very simple. You line up your cards in the order of the written events, and the person who runs out of cards in their hand the quickest wins.
    • Repetition helps students to remember events. After a while, you can have them make their own cards of important events to deepen their learning.

    Recombination Game

  • >>Click for larger image<<

    • TYPE: card game
    • LEVEL: easy
    • TIME: 50min
    • You can recombine the names of famous people.
    • The winner is the person who can combine the names of three separate great men to create the chimera that best matches the requirements. This was conducted as a team competition.
    • The names and descriptions become interesting, so even children who are not good learners can enjoy them. Children who like history can also enjoy guessing the disparate figures.
